## What You Get

### **Quantity Controls**
```
Each Item in Cart Shows:

[−] Button    →  Reduce quantity by 1
    Display   →  Current quantity (e.g., "3")
[+] Button    →  Increase quantity by 1
```

### **Automatic Updates**
- Subtotal recalculates
- Tax recalculates
- Total updates
- ALL INSTANT!

---

## How to Use

### **Step 1: Add Item**
Click [Add to Cart] → Item added with Qty: 1

### **Step 2: Increase Qty**
Click [+] → Qty increases → Price updates → Done!

### **Step 3: Decrease Qty**
Click [−] → Qty decreases → Price updates → Done!
